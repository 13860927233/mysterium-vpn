Mysterium Node Runner (the “Pilot”) description

This pilot is an internal test that we are running over the period of one month with the purpose of gathering data on our network, users and incentive structures. It consists of onboarding both node runners and traffic providers on the Mysterium Network and analysing a set of performance metrics.

We will gradually onboard a certain number of nodes who have been pre-screened through a questionnaire, and we will give them minimum performance requirements. We will simultaneously push traffic through these nodes to analyse node behavior and overall network health. If the minimum requirements are met, the nodes will receive compensation for participating. We will be monitoring their bandwidth performance to ensure that they are meeting our requirements and should they fall outside of the given metrics, they will not receive compensation. In order to incentivize better participation in the pilot, we will have a leaderboard where we will track the top 10 performers. These ‘leaders’ will receive extra benefits during each payment cycle.

Throughout the length of the pilot, we reserve the right to make any changes to the metrics we follow in order to assess the network in a manner that we see fit. We will inform you as soon as any changes are made to the node runner requirements so you can adjust accordingly.

After the one month pilot, we will assess overall performance and will decide on next steps. We cannot ensure that all or any of the participating nodes in the pilot will continue to be used in our network once the Pilot ends. Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for the Pilot, please:

  • fill in the Pilot questionnaire and provide us with an email address where we can contact you should anything go wrong with your payment. Failure to do so will result in our inability to pay you once we start the program if we can’t reach you.
  • be able to download and install node runner software. Although we offer discretionary support, we are not responsible for any issues with your installation during the Pilot.
  • have an Ethereum Wallet address where we can make the payments.
  • comply with node runner software terms & conditions at https://mysterium.network/terms-conditions/

Payments

All payments for top nodes will be made in ETH (Ethereum). You will be responsible for any tax implications related to the payments you receive, as determined by the laws of your jurisdiction of residence or citizenship. Local laws may require us to ask for proof of identity.

You will need to register your ETH Wallet address when you install the node runner software. Payments will be made to this wallet address only. Should you make any mistakes in giving us your wallet address we will be unable to pay you for your service.

Payments will be made on a weekly basis.

We will pay $10 worth of ETH per week for sharing your bandwidth during the Pilot. Here are the minimum requirements to continue to get paid on a weekly basis. Anyone who falls under these standards will not receive payment in the following cycle. Attention: Shutdown and sleep mode will not be able to share. Your operating system has to be active and running the required software and have an active Internet connection.

1. Sharing 1 GB of bandwidth over the course of the week
2. 10 unique sessions during the week

In order to receive an additional $10 worth of ETH per week, you will need to become a top 10 performer on our leaderboard. The conditions for being in the top 10 are as follows: 

1. Minimum 5 GB of data transferred
2. Minimum of 10 h availability per week

The leaderboard can be found at testnet.mysterium.network. This is a way for us to track the top 10 performers in the network based on the criteria above. The more data you share and the more hours you keep your connection running per week, the higher are your chances to become a top 10 node and earn extra compensation. The compensation is capped at $10 worth of ETH per week for each top 10 nodes.

We will also be looking at the following metrics to improve the user experience for nodes but this will not affect how much you can earn:

1. Successful vs unsuccessful connections 
2. Number of unique sessions during the week
3. The average duration of each session

These conditions may change throughout the Pilot and we will inform you as soon as we implement new ones.

Each Sunday at 23:59 EST we will announce the top 10 nodes according to the leaderboard and process payments the following week. This process will be repeated every Sunday throughout the Pilot. The announcement will happen on our Telegram group Mysterium_Network_Nodes. You can always track the leaderboard at the following link: testnet.mysterium.network.

The Pilot is set to run for one month starting on March 18th and will end on April 14th.

If you are disqualified from the Program for any reason, you will NOT receive any payments.

Additional bounties may be earned throughout the Pilot and we will announce them on a rolling basis.
